Intro by Bio

When did it all start?

I have always had a fascination with computers, and building things ever since I was a child.

However, life may not have granted me the best path in getting to where I am right now. I used to be an artist, but my skills were frequently mistaken for that of a designer. This has led me into studying UX design (or Interaction Design) in my Polytechnic Years.

Studying Interaction Design was the catalyst in sparking my interest and passion for coding.

Coding knowledge was relatively limited in my course, but I continued to pursue my interest in it. Most of my skills are self-taught, and are learned from experience while working.

I continued to pursue my studies through Post-Diploma in Information Technology, which broadened my insight to what the IT sector is like, and what other possibilities are out there related to coding.

Why Code?

Building something functional and being able to see it's output is fun and exciting for me. I am always amazed at how lines of words and characters, produce something that's functional.

Coding is also a medium for problem solving, in which I can build something to help me with mundane tasks. For example, a simple folder watching script can help me to quickly organise my files in my computer.

Finally, it is an accomplishment for me to be able to create something functional that will be part of people's lives. Knowing that the code I had contributed will help to solve everyday issues.
